How-To: Juki Sewing Machines

Main Page
  1. Confirm thread, needle, and presser foot for material type.
  2. Set servo motor speed limit for safe startup (200-6000 RPM).
  3. Test stitch on scrap material and tune tension.
  4. Sew final part, guiding fabric evenly without pulling.
  5. Power down machine and clean lint after use.
